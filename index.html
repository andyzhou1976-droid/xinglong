About Our Company

Our Heritage & Growth

Shanxi Xinglong Casting Co., Ltd is a private enterprise established in June 1994. Located in the Yintong Industrial Estate, Xiajiaying Town, Jiaocheng County, Shanxi Province, we have evolved over twenty years into a leader in the industry.

We have successfully integrated a streamlined production chain encompassing smelting, casting, and precision machining processes.

Production Capacity

Our facility currently features:

  • One 6,000 m² manual casting workshop.
  • Two mechanical molding lines.
  • One fully automatic static pressure molding line.
  • Two 158 m² smelting furnaces.

These capabilities enable us to produce 120,000 tons of excellent casting products and 300,000 tons of pig iron per year.

Certifications & Quality

Driven by leading technology and advanced management, we received ISO9001:2000 certification and the Kitemark license issued by BSI in 2001, followed by the SGS product license in 2006. Today, we offer more than 400 certified products to the global market.
